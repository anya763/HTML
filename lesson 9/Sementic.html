Facts about Japan

Japan is country situated in southeren west asia,it is famous for its technology and culture.Because of it's such a varied culture and heritage it attracts many tourists and travelers from all over the world.Besides the culture of Japan it also attracts tourists for its varity of food.one of the most famous dishes which are famous not only in japan but all over the world is Sushi,also known as onigiri.it is a dish made out of mainly rice stuffed with salmon or any other topping of your choice which is topped with seaweed paper.it is eaten with chopstick,which is an wooden stick which is thick at the starting and narrow at the end.
